PTSD Support

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) can affect anyone who has experienced or witnessed a traumatic event. It's important to know that you're not alone, and support is always available.

Common Symptoms:

  • Flashbacks or nightmares
  • Heightened anxiety or hypervigilance
  • Avoidance of triggers
  • Mood swings or emotional numbness
  • Sleep disturbances

Helpful Approaches:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT): Helps reshape negative thought patterns.
  • EMDR Therapy: Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing to process trauma.
  • Mindfulness & Grounding: Techniques to stay present and reduce anxiety.
  • Peer Support: Connect with others who understand your journey.
